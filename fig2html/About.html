About

Companion Dx Consulting, INC, is based in Greater Boston, MA, which is now considered to be the number one Biotech hub in the World. CDXC was founded by George Bashirians, PhD after leaving a successful career in the corporate world to enable him to share his extensive experience in the field of diagnostics and its application to Pharma/Biotech with a wider set of clients.

CDXC’s likely clients are small to medium size companies that may not have the necessary in-house expertise to develop and commercialize Medical Devices. Based on the first-hand and real-world experience of its founder, and extended network of experts, CDXC is able to efficiently and cost-effectively help clients navigate the complex and highly regulated world of Medical Devices.

We don’t just advise how you should do it

We work alongside you to get it done right!

How CDXC can help to create value

We provide support at each phase of the product lifecycle. Starting from creating and implementing a Precision Medicine strategy leading to biomarker discovery, to creating and implementing a Diagnostics strategy leading to device development and commercialization.

Our solutions are customized to meet your specific needs.
